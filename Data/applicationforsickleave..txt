[Recipient's Name]

[Company/Organization Name]

[City, State, Pincode]

Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I am currently unwell and unable to attend work. Due to my illness, I am requesting a sick leave from [start date] to [end date]. I am providing this application to request your approval for my absence from work during this period.

I am suffering from [mention the illness or medical condition, e.g., fever, flu, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take rest and undergo treatment, which I am currently undergoing. I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor, which confirms my illness and recommends the required period of rest.

I apologize for any inconvenience my absence may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am able to return to the office. If there are any pressing tasks that need my attention in the meantime, please let me know and I will do my best to complete them remotely.

I would like to thank you for considering my application. If you require any additional information or documentation, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Enclosures: Medical Certificate

Note: Please modify the application according to your needs and company's policies.
